## Types of tumor

### Benign Tumor

->Non-cancerous
->Capsulates
->Non-invasive
-Slow growing
->Do not metastasize (spread to other parts of the body)
->Cells are normal
![Alt text](image.png)

### Malignant Tumor

->Cancerous
->Non-capsulated
->Metastasize (spread) to other parts of the body
->Cells have large,dark nuclie; may have abnormal shape
